Privacy Notice

At Dr. Roots Dental Clinic, we collect only the information necessary to process appointment requests and respond to contact inquiries. By submitting the appointment or contact form, you consent to this processing as described below.

What We Collect

  • Identity and contact details: name, email address, phone number.
  • Appointment details: preferred date, time slot, and reason for visit.
  • Technical data for security and abuse prevention: IP address, user agent (browser/OS/device), and approximate location (city/region/country) inferred from IP.

How We Use Your Information

  • Schedule, confirm, reschedule, or cancel appointments and send email notifications (including calendar invites).
  • Respond to your inquiries submitted via the contact form.
  • Protect our services from spam and abuse (for example, limiting daily bookings per IP).

Legal Basis

We process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interest in providing clinical services and maintaining the security of our website.

Retention

Appointment and contact records are retained for operational and legal purposes only as long as necessary. Security logs and rate‑limit records are retained for a shorter period.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is used internally by our staff, and by service providers strictly as needed to deliver email notifications and website hosting.

Your Choices

  • You may contact us to correct or request deletion of your information where applicable.
  • You may cancel an appointment at any time by contacting the clinic.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at info@drrootsdc.in.
